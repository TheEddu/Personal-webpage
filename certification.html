CERTIFICATIONS AND COURSES

Cloud Practitioner


The AWS Cloud Practitioner certification demonstrates a foundational understanding of AWS Cloud services, enabling professionals to confidently navigate and utilize cloud technologies for various business applications.

Novo Pentest Profissional - DESEC


O novo Pentest Profissional da DESEC confirma habilidades avançadas em testes de penetração, capacitando os profissionais a identificar, explorar e mitigar vulnerabilidades de segurança em sistemas e redes complexas.

Responsive Web Design


The Responsive Web Design certification from freeCodeCamp validates proficiency in creating websites that adapt seamlessly to different devices and screen sizes, ensuring an optimal user experience across desktops, tablets, and mobile devices.
